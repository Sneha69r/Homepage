Visit Torshov

Enjoy the culture and sports in this vibrant district close to the center of Oslo.

Top three activities to do at Torshov

Attend the show or a concert

The Torshov Theatre is a great place to spend your nightout.

Go for a picnic in the local park

The Torshov Park is perfect for picnic, getting some tan, or chilling out.

Get sweaty at the sports field

Play volleyball, football, basketball or lift some weights.
